No. That's the whole point. You tell us what's eating your week in plain English, we go build it, you look at the results. Most of our clients don't know what "API" stands for and they shouldn't have to.

What if the automation breaks?

We monitor every workflow we build. If something breaks, we fix it within 24 hours at no extra cost — it's already in your monthly retainer. Growth plan and above get a 4-hour response window.

Is there a long contract?

No. Month-to-month. Cancel any time with 14 days' notice. If we don't save you at least 5 hours a week in month one, you don't pay for month two — written into every agreement.

Will my team have to learn new software?

Almost never. We connect the tools you already use — Gmail, QuickBooks, Square, Shopify, Calendly, your CRM. Your team's day-to-day doesn't change; the busywork just disappears.

How fast do you get something running?

Most first workflows are live in 2–3 weeks from signing. Simple ones can be done in 5 days. Complex multi-tool builds take 30–45 days. We tell you the timeline before you pay.
